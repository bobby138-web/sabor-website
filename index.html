Welcome to Sabor Village — A Place Where Tradition, Talent & Nature Thrive

Nestled in the lush highlands of Rangwe in Homa Bay County, Sabor Village stands as a beacon of community, education, and natural beauty. Here, every sunrise brings promise — from young scholars stepping into classrooms, dancers preparing for performance, to forests that hum with life and possibility.

We host annual tournaments, nurture talented youth, and power our community through initiatives like our dam and sustainable practices. Our legacy is carried forward in every act of unity, learning, and love.

Academic Excellence

From a young age, Sabor Village is known for producing students who shine — excelling in school, representing the region, and raising the name of the “Saborian” with pride.

Natural Beauty

With vast forests, serene paths, and the hum of birdlife, our environment is not just scenic — it’s integral to who we are. Nature is in every breath.

Cultural Spirit & Community

Our annual tournaments, dance performances, festivals, and shared celebrations keep the bonds strong and our spirit alive.

Sustainable Progress

Through contributions, local leadership, and shared responsibility — from electric power generation via our dam, to supporting talented youth — we build for tomorrow.
