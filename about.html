Our Story

Sabor was the wise patriarch whose name now echoes in every hill and heart in our community. Married to Anam, he believed the greatest wealth was family — love, unity, and legacy. Together they raised thirteen children, each carrying values of strength, wisdom, and kindness.

The eldest, Onduru Sabor, named after his grandfather, stood out as a leader. With brothers like Okombo, Onduru shared and cared for the land inherited from Sabor. From that inheritance came not just land but the legacy we honor today: the Sabor Family.

Mission

To bring together all Saborians in building a united family that values love, peace, and mutual support — ensuring no one is left behind.

Vision

To see every Saborian successful, united, and empowered within ten years — flourishing in education, relationships, and communal harmony.

Our Values

  • Education & Excellence — We honour learning, celebrate achievement, and support every child's journey to shine.
  • Unity & Brotherhood — As Onduru and Okombo taught, we are strongest together.
  • Respect & Heritage — We preserve our stories, mother tongue, land, and memories of our forebears.
  • Love & Peace — Love binds us; peace guides how we live and treat each other.
